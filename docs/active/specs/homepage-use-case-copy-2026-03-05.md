# Homepage Use-Case Copy (Detailed Draft)

## Purpose

Provide concrete, vivid use-case copy for homepage and brand positioning so visitors can quickly see what Havi does in real family life.

Inspiration source:
- `docs/active/plan/chat-quality-report.json` (route and scenario distribution)

## Messaging principle

For each use case:
1. Start with a real parent moment.
2. Show the conversation input naturally.
3. Show Havi’s response behavior (remember, guide, coordinate, act).
4. End with a practical outcome.

## Use-case set (MVP)

### 1) Fast logging during chaos

Use-case title:
- `Log the moment before it disappears`

Parent moment:
- You are holding a baby, half awake, and need to record sleep/feed/diaper quickly.

Example parent input:
- `Fed 4 oz at 2:10, diaper at 2:25, finally asleep at 2:40.`

What Havi does:
- Captures multiple events in one message.
- Stores timeline context without forms.

Outcome copy:
- `One message captures the whole moment, so you stay present instead of filling out screens.`

### 2) Pattern clarity across days

Use-case title:
- `See what changed, not just what happened`

Parent moment:
- You feel like naps or behavior are shifting but cannot see the pattern.

Example parent input:
- `Can you help me understand why bedtime got harder this week?`

What Havi does:
- Connects recent logs and summarizes signal changes.
- Highlights likely contributors.

Outcome copy:
- `Havi turns scattered moments into a clear pattern you can act on.`

### 3) Next-step guidance in uncertain moments

Use-case title:
- `Get a clear next step when you’re unsure`

Parent moment:
- You have a behavior or transition question and too many conflicting opinions.

Example parent input:
- `Our toddler is melting down at daycare drop-off. What should we try tomorrow?`

What Havi does:
- Gives practical, stepwise suggestions tailored to child context.
- Keeps advice concise and usable in the real moment.

Outcome copy:
- `You move from spiraling to a clear plan in minutes.`

### 4) Shared family alignment

Use-case title:
- `Keep everyone on the same page`

Parent moment:
- Partners and helpers are using different chats and missing updates.

Example parent input:
- `Share today’s summary and tomorrow morning plan with my partner and nanny.`

What Havi does:
- Keeps one shared thread of updates.
- Turns context into a coordinated plan.

Outcome copy:
- `Fewer repeated explanations. Cleaner handoffs. Less friction at home.`

### 5) Memory that persists

Use-case title:
- `Stop carrying everything in your head`

Parent moment:
- Important details get lost between busy days.

Example parent input:
- `Save that she refuses eggs but does better with yogurt in the morning.`

What Havi does:
- Saves durable family memory and makes it retrievable later.

Outcome copy:
- `Havi remembers the details you should not have to re-explain.`

### 6) Task coordination from conversation

Use-case title:
- `Turn insight into action right away`

Parent moment:
- You identify something to do, but tasks get dropped in the shuffle.

Example parent input:
- `Create a reminder to call the pediatrician tomorrow at 9.`

What Havi does:
- Converts conversation into a clear task/reminder.
- Keeps ownership and timing visible.

Outcome copy:
- `Decisions turn into shared follow-through, not forgotten notes.`

### 7) Night-time reassurance

Use-case title:
- `Support at 2 a.m. without digging through tabs`

Parent moment:
- You are exhausted, worried, and need quick orientation.

Example parent input:
- `What was the last feed and diaper?`

What Havi does:
- Surfaces immediate context from recent timeline entries.

Outcome copy:
- `You get the answer fast and move forward calmly.`

### 8) Development and behavior transitions

Use-case title:
- `Navigate transitions with confidence`

Parent moment:
- New routines, regressions, or behavior shifts create uncertainty.

Example parent input:
- `He is waking earlier and skipping one nap. Is this a transition?`

What Havi does:
- Combines trend context with practical transition guidance.

Outcome copy:
- `Havi helps you adjust the plan with less guesswork.`

2. Avoid repeated phrases across adjacent sections.
3. Keep each value statement tied to one parent outcome:
   - save time,
   - reduce mental load,
   - improve alignment,
   - improve decision clarity.
