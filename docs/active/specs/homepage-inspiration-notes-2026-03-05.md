## Patterns Worth Reusing for Havi

1. Huckleberry
   - Clear emotional reassurance + concrete product action in hero.
   - Trust proof near top (`trusted by 5M+ families`) anchors credibility early.
   - "How we help" section converts broad promise into compact pillars.

2. Joy
   - Brand readability is strong (wordmark clarity and hierarchy).
   - Parent-question framing is highly legible and human.
   - Expert-led proof and testimonials make guidance claims feel real.

3. Cactus
   - Strong before/with contrast section makes transformation explicit.
   - Outcome-first message architecture (what changes in daily reality).
   - Clear CTA choreography in hero and repeated close blocks.

4. Simple AI
   - Repeated single core message with capability support blocks.
   - Benefit triad language is concise and skimmable.
   - "How it works" sequence removes ambiguity for first-time visitors.

5. Button Computer
   - Distinct hero thesis + immediate mechanism ("press button, get answer").
   - Minimal, bold typography and generous white space make product intent obvious.
   - Privacy assertion is short, direct, and trust-building.

## Havi Translation (Design + Narrative)

1. Hero needs one clear thesis + one concrete mechanism sentence.
2. Add earlier trust proof above the fold (expert-informed + literature-grounded).
3. Keep a visible before/with module close to top.
4. Increase white-space rhythm between narrative bands (avoid dense card stacking).
5. Simplify visual framing on product images (one dominant frame; reduce nested borders).
6. Strengthen logo/wordmark visibility and lockup scale in top nav.
